M.A. in Museology and Cultural Heritage

Elevate your expertise with the M.A. in Museology and Cultural Heritage, a dynamic programme blending art history, cultural studies, and cutting-edge digital technologies like virtual reality and AI. This interdisciplinary degree prepares you to address contemporary challenges such as decolonization, inclusivity, and sustainability, equipping you to lead and innovate in museums and cultural institutions worldwide.

With a fully online format and hands-on internships at prestigious heritage organizations, gain practical experience alongside academic knowledge in this 1-year, 60-credit programme. Offered by JGU, an Institution of Eminence, this programme prepares you for dynamic careers as curators, exhibition designers, and digital heritage experts.
